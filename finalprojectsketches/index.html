Challenge

The challenge for this assignment is to create 5 different sketches for our final project, and we ae to do this assignment individually before coming together as a group to discuss our ideas and choose the best ones out of ALL of our sketches.

Problems

I didn't have any issues with this assignment since it was just creating really fast sketches of ideas for our final project. It was a bit hard to try to create interesting ideas, but I felt as though there was only so much to do with our final project idea to keep it interesting and not look like a simple information page.

Final Result

I think that even though the sketches that I did are pretty simple and look similar to each other, I think that they're pretty cute and unique compared to other websites. Our team came up with super cute ideas and I think we all have a similar plan in mind, so I am very excited to get together with them in order to discuss our ideas and create one together :)

Movie Idea 1

This is my first sketch of my first idea! The boxes would be screencaps of specific movies, and once you click on the photo, it'll take you to a whole other page filled with information on the location as well as fun facts about the movie/location!

Movie Idea 2

This is my second idea. It'll be like taking a tour bus or a road trip to the different locations! Each road sign will be the location/entrance to the specific location, and once you click on the roadsign, it'll give information on the movie as well as fun facts.

Movie Idea 3

For this idea, I wanted to sketch out a little treasure map! It's pretty self explanatory, but I'm still unsure on what to put for each movie location or how it should be identified. I was just focusing on the map itself.

Movie Idea 4

I think it'd be cute if we did a little website based on the Hollywood Walk of Fame, but instead of actors or singers, we put the movies instead! Each star will be a movie/location, and once you click on the star, it'll lead you to another page with more information.

Movie Idea 5

I wanted to do something similar to the treasure map, but put icons that represent the movie instead of a photo. Once you click on the icon, it'll take you to a different page discussing the location of the film as well as other movies that were filmed there.
